  • THE MADRAS SEVA SADAN

    Tambaram

    The Seva Sadan has always been attentive to the needs of the community. In response to an earnest appeal from the people of Tambaram, Chennai, the founderâ€™s setup a co-educational school in 1953 which in course of time has developed into an aided Higher Secondary School catering to students from the LKG to the Twelfth Standard.

    The Sir & Lady M Venkatasubba Rao school was started to provide quality education in the English in the Tambaram Locality. Expanding on this vision, the Seva Sadan, in conjunction with the Madras Round Table 42, has built a block of brand-new classrooms to provide impetus to the English Medium School. The Sponsor, was inspired to donate to this Noble cause after experiencing first-hand the support provided by the Seva Sadan Schools, for their special needs niece.
